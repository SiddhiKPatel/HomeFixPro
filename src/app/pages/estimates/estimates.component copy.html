How much does house cleaning cost?

Jun 28, 2022

The national average cost of house cleaning is $40-$65 per hour, per cleaner. The total price depends on the size of the home, type of cleaning and where you live. A three-bedroom, 2,000-square-foot home costs $150 to $250 to clean on average, while a one-bedroom apartment starts at $80 to $110. However, the overall average cost of house cleaning is $165.
